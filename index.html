Hello, my name is Dan!

I am a Software developer and I currently work at Acima Credit in Sandy Utah. When I'm not working I love to build things and work on new projects. I have a beautiful wife Sara who is a Grapic Designer for a company in Salt Lake, and an artist. Our hobbies are to go to art shows and conventions to sell art. We have a daughter Juniper and one on the way.

I got into coding because I love to see a blank space and turn it into a kingdom. I think that developers hold one of the most powerful abilities; they get to shape the world. I started my coding journey at a coding bootcamp in 2016 then student taught there for almost a year. Wanting more experience on real projects, my wife and I started a business called Kuma-Ko building websites for small businesses. Moving on from there we both entered the work force.

Please feel free to look through my site and message me if you have any questions.
